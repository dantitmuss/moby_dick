A New Way of Reading The Whale

Moby Dick; or, The Whale, is the entire world. Between the cover of the novel, you can find any reason, explanation, and humor on anything. Which is why I created the Moby Dick Bible. It’s a new way of consuming Herman Melville’s book that treats it like a sacred text, to find meaning and guidance.


It works by first creating a brand new verse in the style of Melville’s beautiful, majestic, weird prose, then by finding appropriate verses within the book. If you are a technical dork, you can find out more or get the code from GitHub.


Enter anything here, and the Moby Dick Bible will give you an appropriate verse from the good book. So type something in! Revenge, the mystery of the open ocean, the movie Pulp Fiction… anything.
